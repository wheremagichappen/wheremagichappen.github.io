Journey

Our 2019 Senior Project

  • September 2018

    Our Journey Begin

    We were in the same Intro to VR class and we decided to do something with VR. We wanted to push further on what we had learned. We began researching for a lot of VR project and we discovered VR projects with haptic feedback such as VR bone surgery with robot arm that provides haptic feedback. We knew right away what we wanted to do.

  • January 2019

    Our first milestone

    After our first semester digging in VR case studies. We finally decided to make a compelling virtual experience. We succeedeed in making our first 3D model and scaled it to the correct size. We had our first iteration: a chair that can change color whenever user sit down; a freezing terrain with self-made plant. We watched a bunch of tutorial and did several trials before having a complete working iteration. We were proud of our selves.

  • Febuary 2019

    Atlas Expo

    We had our project 99% done. We presented at Atlas Expo. The kids in Expo loved it. They loved fighting the dragon. We received a lot of constructive feedback.

  • May 2019

    Finish Project

    We have a fully functional project. Here is our documentation
