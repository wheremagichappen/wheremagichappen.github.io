Where Magic Happen?

Where Magic Happens is a Hyper Reality experience where players can touch and feel the virtual world. Where Magic Happen is fun and interactive. Everything you can touch in this magical world can manipulate or trigger different events that impact the virtual world around you.

Hyper Reality

Virtual Object scale to the real size in the real world. Players can touch and feel what they see

Sensor Triggered Events

We place sensors inside the scene so that whatever objects player interact with. There is a chance they will trigger events in the Virtual World. The Magical World will become happier or scarier? Play and Figure out!

RPG Element

We have game elements like fighting dragon, opening chest, destroying treasures.
